Introducing Luggage for Her by Zouk.


It all started with a vision: To bring stylish and functional products with an essence of India, to the world.



All large things start with some small situations. In our case, it happened when Disha visited the famous white desert of Kutch in Gujarat. Kutch is also known for local handicraft products. Her IIMA batchmates clicked pics for Instagram but didn't buy anything. They said these products didn't fit the modern sensibilities.



Thus, the idea of Zouk was born. We realized a gap for stylish All bags are handcrafted by expert artisans. In addition to our online presence, Zouk is now available offline and more. Our products are a great choice for office goers, homemakers and college students. Many people also choose our products for gifts in Weddings, Anniversary, and functional modern products with an essence of India. For the world.

Zouk is a 100% Vegan & Proudly Indian brand. We make laptop bags, handbags, women's office bags, female wallets, mini wallets, sling bags, bucket bags, backpacks, travel pouches for women & men. We use Ikat fabric and Jute Khadi fabric to make vegan leather bags and other vegan products. All bags are handcrafted by expert artisans. In addition to our online presence, Zouk is now available offline and more. Our products are a great choice for office goers, homemakers and college students. Many people also choose our products for gifts in Weddings, Anniversary, Diwali, Holi, Christmas, New Year, Karwa Chauth, Valentine's Day, Women's Day. The company was founded by IIT & IIM alumni. All products are PeTA Approved Vegan. Zouk is a brand under Sea Turtle Private Limited company.
